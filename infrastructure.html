Infrastructure Vulnerabilities

Extreme heat poses significant challenges to critical infrastructure systems. Transportation networks, power grids, and water systems all face increased stress during heat waves, potentially leading to failures that can cascade through interconnected systems.

Key infrastructure impacts include:

  • Power grid strain and potential blackouts
  • Transportation system disruptions
  • Water system stress and quality issues
  • Building and construction material degradation

Critical Systems at Risk

Different infrastructure systems respond to heat stress in various ways. Understanding these vulnerabilities is crucial for developing effective adaptation and resilience strategies.
